Owners Manual Benjamin Bell My style: • I am motivated by self-improvement in order to become capable and become less of a liability • I enjoy feeling capable and useful in my environment. I love to learn because that makes me more valuable to a team. • I am highly motivated as well by my need to provide for my wife. I also want to be well established so that I can be of service to my family and friends if need be. My values: • Skills – I want to become as well rounded as possible so that I am always somewhat helpful. • Knowledge – knowledge is power. It allows oneself to improve. • Justice – Fair is fair. We need to understand the context of any event in order to be fair and just to all participants. • Respect – Respect is the best way to bring a group together, as well as to become comfortable with that group. Health: I am very much into fitness as I believe that when I am healthy and taking care of myself that I will be able to work harder, longer, and better. Mental and emotional health are just as important as physical health. I like to think that I am decent at making sure that I am healthy in all ways. Work: I am a firm believer that if I want to succeed in life and be happy, I have to work smart and hard. I regard all work as good work if I am learning from my experiences. Personal life: I love my family and friends very much. The work I do is almost always done so that I am capable of supporting my family and friends when I need to. Workview: My workview is that all experience is good experience, and good work is always worth doing. I value independence, learning and an open environment. I will work whatever job I need to become a more valuable individual. Energy: My energy is one of fun, responsibility and service. I love to have fun and make sure everyone is included. I’d like to think that I will always take responsibility for my own actions as well as take on new responsibility when asked. I will always help someone when they ask me to help them.
